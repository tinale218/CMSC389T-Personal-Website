About Me

Hi, my name is Tina! I am currently a senior at the University of Maryland, College Park majoring in Computer Science with a minor in General Business.

This past summer I interned at T. Rowe Price in the Global Technology business unit. I mostly developed in JavaScript and ReactJS to create frontend UI enhancements to internal facing applications supporting the Individual Investor's Retirement Advisory Service. I also analyzed Retirement Advisory Service (RAS) driver metrics to identify key drivers leading to greater RAS Enrollments for the business. After graduation, I will be returning to T. Rowe Price full time as a software engineer.

On campus, I am an organizer for Technica, the largest hackathon for underrrepresented genders in tech hosted annually at UMD. I am also involved in Kappa Theta Pi, a co-ed tech fraternity. Part time, I work as a CS Student Ambassador leading academic workshops, advising sessions, and providing insights to prospective undergraduate students. In my free time, I enjoy spending time with my nieces + nephews, watching movies/shows, and trying new restaurants or recipes!
